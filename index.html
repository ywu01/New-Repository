BIO

I'm a rising senior at Harvard majoring in Economics and Computer Science. I interned at Zinc, a Y-Combinator backed e-commerce startup, last summer and currently will be back in the Bay Area for the summer of 2016. During this year's spring semester, I've worked along with Daniel Wu and a few others on their President's Challenge entry, WelcomeHome, which got to the final round. I've also been a photographer for Kirkland House for the past two years and have served as a multimedia executive for The Harvard Crimson between my sophomore/junior years.

I'm interested in the intersection of business, product, and design. My resume offers some additional details.

ZINC TECHNOLOGIES

As a business analyst, I did a mix of business development, marketing, sales, and support. Typical for a company of small size, as was the case with Zinc. Specifically worked with support for PriceYak, an automatic repricer, and growth for JoeLister. zinc.io

DATA VIZ

The final project for CS171, a course about data visualization. This particular visualization draws data from the Spotify API, and creates a visual network of artists based on an initial query. Double clicking nodes will lead you to the artist at that particular node; using the toolbar in the middle will also allow you to shuffle, play different songs, and navigate. Worked in a team of three. Hosted on Github.
